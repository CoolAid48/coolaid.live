# COOLAID'S HALL OF FAME

### What is the HOF?
The Hall of Fame is a build dedicated to CoolAid's top supporters who have been most active members of the community. You need 3 or more Days of Watchtime to apply for a HOF plot. (Example of a plot shown below)

### Hall of Fame FAQ

#### Q. How can I apply for the HOF  once I am eligible?

You can find the application form [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdO8tFqPqHWK9bx5z2iTWX2G8-tafWylViIJnnAZE4twjISQw/viewform)!

Questions are self-explanatory, and will be validated before accepted.



#### Q. How is my HOF plot laid out?

All plots are given the same basic layout:

- 15 Item Frames to display items
- Armor Stand to display armor or to hold items/heads
- Barrel under the Armor Stand to store extra items after the item frames are filled 
- Shelves to put miniblocks on 
- Space on the wall for custom painting, banners, or extra miniblocks
- A "display case" on the side for specific special items (see the spreadsheet)
- Space on the floor for pets and even more miniblocks


#### Q. How do I add items to my plot?

All available blocks and items can be redeemed with Twitch channel points, which are earned just by watching stream! Some blocks and items cost more than others.

You can find the spreadsheet of every single item [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/145x9910pUwjP6y4u008SjjX_q6jKdTfD7xz55zppjpg/edit?gid=0#gid=0).


#### Q. What if I run out of space to display items?

Every plot has a free barrel under their armor stand to store extra items. You can also redeem even more barrels for even more storage if you need to!
