### TurtleGang

TurtleGang is about supporting all playstyles. Minecraft is about having fun and the way you play the game does not matter. If you are having a good time, you are winning and the turtle helmet is symbol for that. TurtleGang members are people who don’t gatekeep the way minecraft is played, so if that sounds like you sit back and enjoy this awesome creator.
